About Dataset
About this Dataset
This dataset explores the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and higher education in 2026. As AI tools become standard in every classroom, I created this synthetic data to help fellow students and researchers analyze the real-world impact of LLMs on academic performance and mental well-being.

Key Features:
Academic Metrics: Baseline GPA vs. Post-AI GPA.
Student Behavior: Primary tools used (Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot) and daily usage hours.
Trendy Insights: Ethics concerns and "AI Career Readiness" scores.
Potential Use Cases:
Predict a student's career confidence based on their AI usage.
Analyze which majors are adopting AI tools the fastest.
Build a regression model to see the impact of AI on GPA.



kaggle: Developer Burnout Prediction Dataset(7000 Samples)
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/asifxzaman/developer-burnout-prediction-dataset7000-samples
About Dataset
📊 Overview
This dataset models developer work patterns, stress levels, and burnout risk based on lifestyle and productivity factors.

It captures realistic relationships between workload, health habits, and mental stress.

🎯 Objective
Predict burnout level (Low/Medium/High)
Analyze impact of work-life balance
Perform classification tasks
📁 Features
age: Developer age
experience_years: Years of experience
daily_work_hours: Work hours per day
sleep_hours: Sleep duration
caffeine_intake: Cups per day
bugs_per_day: Bugs produced
commits_per_day: Git commits
meetings_per_day: Meetings attended
screen_time: Total screen time
exercise_hours: Physical activity
stress_level: Stress score (0–100)
burnout_level: Target variable
⚙️ Dataset Characteristics
7000 rows
Includes missing values (~2%)
Realistic feature dependencies
Suitable for classification and analysis
💡 Use Cases
Burnout prediction models
Workplace productivity analysis
Health & lifestyle data science projects
